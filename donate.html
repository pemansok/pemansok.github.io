We raise funds to support the activities and opportunities provided by THAMES and the Saturday Music Centre.

If you would like to help by making a donation we would be very grateful.


Shop and contribute


It’s very simple – just visit http://www.easyfundraising.org.uk/causes/thamessmc/ to register and shop with over 2000 well known retailers like Amazon, Argos, M&S, eBay and many more. Whenever you buy something, the retailer makes a donation to THAMES’ friends. They’ve helped other causes raise over £2,000,000, so it really does work.


We would be grateful if you could use easyfundraising too. It doesn’t cost you a penny extra to shop through easyfundraising and you can even save money with special offers and voucher codes.


So please make a difference to Thames Saturday Music Centre and take a look at easyfundraising today.
